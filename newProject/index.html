Who the Buddha was

The Buddha taught that the clinging of mind is the cause of suffering. We are ignorant of our perceptions and we cling to things that we like and we are averse to those things that we do not like. The surest way to liberate oneself is to be a light unto oneself and to learn to see clearly by walking the Noble Eightfold Path which advocates for:

  1. Right Thinking
  2. Right Speech
  3. Right View
  4. Right Action
  5. Right Mindfulness
  6. Right Concentration
  7. Right Livlihood
  8. Right Diliegence
